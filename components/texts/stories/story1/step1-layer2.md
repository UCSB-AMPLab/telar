---
title: Francisco Maldonado y Mendoza
---

Born in Spain in 1551, Francisco Maldonado y Mendoza moved to Santa Fe de Bogotá in 1583. After marrying Jerónima de Orrego in 1586, he began purchasing estates in the Bogotá savannah. By the mid-1590s, he became encomendero of the Bogotá Indigenous community and owned one of the most prosperous cattle ranches in the New Kingdom of Granada. His purchases of estates were made possble by resettlement of local communities into [[reducción|reducciones]] with a [[resguardo|resguardos]].
