The grasslands represented in the *Painting* are probably not the same ones that cover the savanna today. In the eighteenth century, herders began importing African grass varieties that produced better grazing lands for cattle, though it is possible that these types of grasses had begun to spread in the New World previously since they were used for bedding on ships used in the slave trade.
